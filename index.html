About Chic.

Eating out with friends is nice, but you remember why you don’t do it more often when it comes down to splitting the check. It was a continuous struggle for us top and that is why we came up with a simple solution — money pooling. You join a check, tap a button, and off you go.und out they value time above all. The faster they can clear tables, the faster they can seat more people. From there, the idea evolved into an end-to-end platform for restaurants and groups that can efficiently and intuitively eliminate their struggles.

Services we offer

We run on a Software as a Service (SasS) model, where restaurants pay a monthly subscription fee to gain access to our platform. We provide them with a customized menu and interface, one that helps track orders, bills, and payments

Customers, on the other hand, can download the mobile app for free. They can join the restaurant’s electronic tab and decide on the split method (equally or per dish). Everything happens in-app, with credit card information securely saved on file for the customers.

This eliminates the wait time for the check, providing a seamless experience for the customers and streamlined process for the restaurant.
