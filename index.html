About Chic

Eating out with friends is nice, but you remember why you don’t do it more often when it comes down to splitting the check. It was a continuous struggle for us top and that is why we came up with a simple solution — Chic. Here's how it works:

  • Open Chic
  • Insert your table's code number to get immediate access to your order
  • Select what dishes you'd like to pay for
  • Confirm Payment!

    Services we offer

    We run on a Software as a Service (SasS) model, where restaurants pay a monthly subscription fee to gain access to our platform. We provide them with a customized menu and interface, one that helps track orders, bills, and payments

    Customers, on the other hand, can download the mobile app for free or redirect to our website. They can join the restaurant’s electronic tab and decide on the split method (equally or per dish). Everything happens in-app and on-web, with credit card information securely saved on file for the customers.

    This eliminates the wait time for the check, providing a seamless experience for the customers and streamlined process for the restaurant.
